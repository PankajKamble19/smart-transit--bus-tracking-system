🎓 Bus Management System - Learn More for Students

About the System

The Bus Management System for Students helps students easily check bus routes, timings, and stop details. It ensures every student knows their bus schedule and travels safely and on time.

Key Features

🗺️ Route Information

Check your assigned bus routes, stops, and destinations in a clear and visual format.

🕒 Daily Schedule

Displays updated bus arrival and departure times to keep you on track every day.

👩‍🎓 Student Access

Log in to see your bus number, pickup time, and driver details securely.

🚍 Real-Time Updates

Get instant notifications about delays, route changes, or new schedules.

📋 Bus Details

View information like driver name, contact number, and vehicle registration.

💡 User-Friendly Design

Modern, clean, and easy-to-use interface designed especially for students.

Benefits for Students

  • View bus route and stop details anytime.
  • Stay informed about any changes instantly.
  • Helps manage time efficiently.
  • Reduces confusion and waiting time.
  • Safer and smarter travel management.
