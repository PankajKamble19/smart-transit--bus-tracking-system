Privacy Policy

Your privacy and safety are important to us.

Bus Transit System respects your privacy and keeps your personal information secure. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data while using our transport services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Student name, contact number, and route details
  • Driver information and bus tracking details
  • Attendance records
  • Admin or user login details

2. How We Use Information

  • To manage bus routes and student records
  • To monitor attendance
  • To improve safety and transport services
  • To send important updates or alerts

3. Data Protection

We use secure systems to protect your information and prevent unauthorized access.

4. Information Sharing

Personal information is not sold or shared with unauthorized people. It is only used for transport management and safety purposes.

5. GPS Tracking

Live location is used only for bus tracking, route monitoring, and student safety.

6. User Responsibility

  • Provide correct information
  • Keep login credentials secure
  • Use the system responsibly

7. Policy Changes

Privacy terms may be updated when necessary for better service and security.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact Bus Transit System Administration.
